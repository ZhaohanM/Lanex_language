2. Cookies (but not the yummy ones)
A cookie is a small text file that is stored on a user's computer for record-keeping purposes. Cookies can take the form of session cookies or persistent cookies. A session cookie expires when you close your browser and is used to make it easier for you to navigate our website. A persistent cookie remains on your hard drive for a specified period of time. We may use cookies to provide you with a better user experience by tracking your use of our Site, the links you click, and the searches you conduct on our Site. You may, at any time, delete by modifying your browser settings but keep in mind that some of our Services may not function properly with such settings.
